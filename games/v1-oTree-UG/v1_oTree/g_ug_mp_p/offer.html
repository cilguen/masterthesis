Ultimatum Game Overview - Variation B

  • Stage 1: Players P1 and P2 propose how to split the bonus payment of 6£ with Player R.
    • The final offer is then calculated as the average of both offers. Both Players P1 and P2 must send the amount of the final offer, not how much they proposed, even if the final amount is more or less than their own proposal.
  • Stage 2: Player R can either accept or reject the proposed split.
    • If Player R accepts, all Players get money according to Player P1 and P2's final offer.
    • If Player R rejects, all Players receive no bonus payment (0£).

    You are providing strategies for all possible offers of Players P1 and P2, not actively negotiating.

    Responder Instructions:

  • You are Player P1 in this Game.
  • Specify how you would split the bonus payment with Player R.
  • There are no right or wrong answers. The study is aimed at finding your personal preferences.
