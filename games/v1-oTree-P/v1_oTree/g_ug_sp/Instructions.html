Ultimatum Game Overview - Variation A

  • Stage 1: Player P proposes how to split the bonus payment of 4£ with Player R.
  • Stage 2: Player R can either accept or reject the proposed split.
    • If Player R accepts, they both get money according to Player P's proposal.
    • If Player R rejects, both players receive nothing (0£).

    You are providing strategies for all possible offers of Player P, not actively negotiating.

    Responder Instructions:

  • You are Player R in this Game.
  • Specify whether you would accept or reject offers based on different offer scenarios.
  • There are no right or wrong answers. The study is aimed at finding your personal preferences.
