Welcome to our Research Survey


Among all the participants who complete the survey and pass the attention checks, some participants will be selected to play for a bonus payment of up to 6£. At the end of the experiment you will get to know if you have played for the bonus payment.

Please be assured that your information will be handled with the utmost care and security. Your data will be collected for research purposes only. The data will be handled only in Germany on devices owned and/or used by the researcher. No personal data is collected in the context of this study. By clicking "Next" you consent to your anonymized data being used in the context of this experiment. If you do not consent to your data being used in the context of this experiment, you can close this page now and thereby exit the survey before any data is collected.

You have been selected to play the role of Player R. Out of all the participants in this study, one Player R's strategies will be implemented for task 2 and another Player R's strategies will be implemented for task 3.
